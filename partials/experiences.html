Learn More About Our Teacher Training Programs

Our programs are very affordable with flexible schedules to fit anyone’s lifestyle. Located in New York’s buzzing Hell’s Kitchen, we create a super-friendly, non-intimidating community where anyone could learn yoga and “fit-in.” Our students come from widely varying backgrounds, from inner-city social workers to lifelong practicing yogis. We’ve even had a number of pregnant women and students with physical challenges complete the program. No doubt, you’ll work very hard, but you’ll also be surrounded by an energetic community which often leads to lifelong friendships.
